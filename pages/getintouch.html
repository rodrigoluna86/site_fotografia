About

Raised in Villa Carlos Paz, Argentina, Diego Luna first picked up a camera at age twenty. After graduating college he moved to Córdoba and started law studies at Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, but the impulse to take more and more photo was stronger...and decided to pause (probably abandon) his studies, to dedicate full time to photography.

Growing up with the rise of social media and digital techniques enabled Diego to see the bigger picture of a growing industry. He enjoys creating emotive imagery, inspired by feminine strength and unique beauty.

Nowadays Diego has worked for more than ten years in both the national and international fashion and advertising market, creating a solid base of customers all over the world. Whether on the set or on a motorcycle, he is always with a camera, a lens and the will to take one more photo.
